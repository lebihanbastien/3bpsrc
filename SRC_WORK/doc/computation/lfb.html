-------------------------------------------------------------------------%
 lfb(manifold_branch_stable, cr3bp,  earth, moon, user, params,  cst,  varargin)

 lfb computes an LEO-to-EML2 Halo transfer via a differential correction procedure on the 
 distance to the desired LEO altitude. The procedure makes use of the
 interior manifold to flyby the Moon.
